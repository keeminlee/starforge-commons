# GOVERNANCE_MAP — which document rules what

The cold-read entry surface for anyone (agent or human) trying to find the authoritative answer. Precedence, always: **CANON → RECURSOR → room-local**. A skill can never override the law; a room can never override either.

## The shape of authority

- **CANON/** is law. Amended only by ratified gold.
- **RECURSOR/** is method — procedures that implement the law. Amended by gold too, but expected to evolve faster.
- **PULSE/** is motion — the ladder's working directories. Bronzes/silvers/golds are filings, not authority.
- **MEEPS/** is the people — rooms, the dorm manual, the ledger. Each room is authoritative about exactly one thing: its own occupant, in their own voice.
